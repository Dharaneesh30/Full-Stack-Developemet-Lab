About DreamPillow

Wellness Through Better Rest

DreamPillow is designed to support users who want healthier sleep habits and a more peaceful bedtime routine. It combines helpful sleep tools with relaxing media in one simple interface.

The application focuses on comfort, routine, and mindfulness so that users can reduce stress, improve rest, and begin each morning with more energy and focus.

Sleep Tips

  • Maintain a regular sleep schedule
  • Reduce screen time before bed
  • Listen to calming music
  • Keep your room cool
